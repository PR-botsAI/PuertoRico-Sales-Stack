# Jeb Blount

**Born:** 1967  
**Category:** Modern Sales Influencer  
**Status:** Founder of Sales Gravy; author of *Fanatical Prospecting*, *Sales EQ*, *Objections*, *Inked*, *Virtual Selling*; among the most-booked sales speakers of the 2020s

---

## Core Philosophy

> "The number one reason for failure in sales is an empty pipeline. The number one reason for an empty pipeline is reluctance to prospect."

Blount's premise is that sales discipline — not talent, charisma, or product — is the durable differentiator. Specifically, the daily discipline of prospecting separates the careers that compound from the careers that plateau.

## Signature Frameworks

- **The 30-Day Rule** — What you do this week shows up 30, 60, 90 days from now.
- **The Law of Replacement** — You must replace every deal that closes or dies, daily.
- **Five Cs of social selling** — Connect, Curate, Create, Curiosity, Consistency.
- **Sales EQ** — Emotional intelligence as the dominant modern sales skill.
- **Multi-channel prospecting** — Phone + email + LinkedIn + video + referral, sequenced.

## Speaking Style

Intense, direct, no-nonsense, and tactically dense. Blount packs every keynote with immediately-implementable techniques, and his energy reflects 30 years of sales-floor reality.

## Why Puerto Rico Sellers Should Study Blount

Blount's prospecting discipline is the cure for the most common career-killer among local PR reps: the second-year slump, when personal-network momentum ends and systematic outreach becomes necessary. His framework gives reps a literal daily calendar block that, executed for 12 weeks, transforms their pipeline.

## Legacy

Blount is the modern Bettger — the writer who reasserted that consistent daily activity, rigorously tracked, is still the unsexy core of every sustainable sales career.
