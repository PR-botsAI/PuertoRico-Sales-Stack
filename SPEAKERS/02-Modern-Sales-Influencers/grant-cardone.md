# Grant Cardone

**Born:** 1958  
**Category:** Modern Sales Influencer  
**Status:** Founder of Cardone Capital and Cardone Training Technologies; one of the most polarizing and effective sales motivators of the 2010s–2020s

---

## Core Philosophy

> "Average is a failing formula."

Cardone's premise: most people fail not from lack of talent but from setting goals at the level of *acceptable* effort. The 10X Rule argues that targets should be 10 times what feels reasonable — and the action taken to reach them must also scale by 10x. Anything less guarantees mediocrity.

## Signature Frameworks

- **The 10X Rule** — Targets and effort, both multiplied by ten.
- **Massive Action** — Four levels: do nothing, retreat, normal action, massive action. Only the fourth produces breakthrough.
- **Obsession as virtue** — Reframe "too intense" as a competitive moat.
- **Sell or Be Sold** — Life is a constant sales conversation; develop the muscle or be flattened by those who have.
- **No retreat language** — Backup plans become destinations.

## Key Works

## Speaking Style

Loud, brash, confrontational, and energetically high-intensity. Cardone is the modern American sales speaker most associated with raw aggression and self-promotion. His delivery is intentionally polarizing — reps either love it or reject it outright.

## Why Puerto Rico Sellers Should Study Cardone

The Cardone *quantity* doctrine — more calls, more meetings, more follow-ups — is universally applicable and especially useful for breaking PR reps out of comfort-zone performance plateaus. The Cardone *style* (high-volume, status-loaded, confrontational) should be cultural-filtered for PR contexts, where pure mainland aggression can damage *confianza*. Take the action; calibrate the volume.

## Legacy

Cardone reinjected high-intensity volume doctrine into a sales industry that had drifted toward soft-skills emphasis. Love him or hate him, his Massive Action framework has produced thousands of millionaires in real estate, auto, and SaaS.
