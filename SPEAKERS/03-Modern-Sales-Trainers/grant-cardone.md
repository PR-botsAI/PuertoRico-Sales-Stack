# Grant Cardone

**Era:** 2008–present · **Legacy:** Activity-multiplication doctrine · **Section:** Modern Sales Trainers

## Overview

Grant Cardone built his early career in automotive sales training, scaled into a real-estate investment portfolio reportedly valued in the billions (multifamily, primarily Florida and Texas), and over the last fifteen years became the most aggressively-branded sales personality in the English-speaking internet. He runs Cardone Capital, Cardone University, and the 10X Growth Conference, and publishes daily across social platforms.

## Core philosophy

**The 10X Rule:** set targets 10 times higher than what you think possible, and apply 10 times more action than you think necessary. The doctrine is intentionally extreme — Cardone argues that average sellers fail because they set realistic goals and apply realistic effort, hitting realistic (mediocre) outcomes. Multiplying both inputs is the way out of the average band.

## Signature methods

- **10X targets + 10X action.** Pair both; one without the other is fantasy or grind.
- **Massive Action levels:** No Action, Retreat, Average Action, Massive Action. Most sellers oscillate between Retreat and Average. Massive is the only level that produces outliers.
- **The Obsession is the price.** Identify what you're willing to be obsessed with; obsession is the actual unlock, not motivation.
- **No-excuses framework.** Catalog and refuse every common excuse before it appears.
- **Sell-or-be-sold.** Every interaction is a transaction of belief; passivity is just losing more slowly.

## Puerto Rico application

Cardone is **a sharp tool that needs careful handling in PR**. The 10X activity principle is universally true and transferable: a PR seller running 10 weekly outreach hours will outperform one running 1, regardless of cultural context. **However**, Cardone's *aesthetic* — supercars, mansions, jet videos, hard-edge bravado — reads as *fanfarronería* in many segments of PR business culture, where wealth is traditionally signaled with restraint, not display. Recommendation: use Cardone's **methodology** (targets, action levels, sequencing) and downplay his **persona** when training PR sellers calling on family-business owners and professional service buyers. For metro real-estate, automotive, and high-velocity B2C sales, the full Cardone register works fine. For consultative B2B and family-business sales, take the discipline and leave the costume.
