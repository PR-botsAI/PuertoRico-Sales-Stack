# 03 — Modern Sales Trainers

The three voices currently defining how sales is taught in the 2020s. Where the Classic Legends and Motivational Giants laid foundation, this section is **applied 2026 practice**: short-form video distribution, paid acquisition math, omnichannel sequencing, offer engineering, and the seller-as-creator hybrid.

If the Classic Legends taught you how to behave in the room, and the Motivational Giants taught you how to stay in the game, this section teaches you **how to scale activity and engineer offers in the current digital environment.**

## Profiles in this section

- **[Grant Cardone](./grant-cardone.md)** — The 10X advocate. Activity multiplication as identity.
- **[Jeb Blount](./jeb-blount.md)** — The prospecting authority. Modern phone, email, social, and video sequences operationalized.
- **[Alex Hormozi](./alex-hormozi.md)** — The offer engineer. Value Equation and Core Four channel framework that anchors most current B2C/coaching/SaaS funnels.

## Why this section is short

Three people, not ten. The bar is: *currently teaching, currently shipping work, currently moving the public conversation about selling.* Many trainers monetize a single past breakthrough — we excluded them. These three publish weekly, evolve their material, and have measurable impact on contemporary practice.

## How to use this section

- **Cardone** for activity standard and disposition.
- **Blount** for the actual mechanics of pipeline generation.
- **Hormozi** for everything upstream of the sales call: offer, traffic, lead capture, conversion math.

Apply them in that order if you're building a modern sales practice from scratch.

## Puerto Rico relevance

- **Cardone:** high-energy register, somewhat polarizing locally; works for ambitious metro-area sellers; can read as *exagerado* in conservative buyer segments.
- **Blount:** universally applicable; his omnichannel methodology adapts directly to the WhatsApp-dominant PR communication environment.
- **Hormozi:** offer engineering is the single highest-leverage skill for PR entrepreneurs and coaches operating in a price-sensitive market — his Value Equation reframes margin compression as a value-stacking problem.
