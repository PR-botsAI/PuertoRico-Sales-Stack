# Les Brown

**Born:** 1945  
**Category:** Motivational Speaker  
**Status:** One of the most powerful platform speakers of the 20th–21st centuries; voice of the "It's possible" generation

---

## Core Philosophy

> "You don't have to be great to get started, but you have to get started to be great."

Brown rose from being labeled "educable mentally retarded" as a child to becoming one of the most paid speakers in America. His philosophy is grounded in radical self-belief, the rejection of internalized limitation, and the conviction that ordinary people contain extraordinary capacity.

## Signature Themes

- **"It's possible."** The signature phrase that anchors his philosophy.
- **The graveyard is the richest place on earth** — buried with potential never expressed.
- **Hunger over talent** — Hungry, willing people beat talented, complacent ones, always.
- **Self-talk as destiny** — The inner dialogue you tolerate becomes the life you live.
- **The OQP framework** — Only Quality People in your inner circle.

## Speaking Style

Voice-as-instrument, soaring, deliberately rhythmic, and emotionally cathartic. Brown's keynotes are designed to physically move audiences — to break years of internalized doubt in 60 minutes of immersive delivery.

## Why Puerto Rico Sellers Should Study Les Brown

For PR sellers facing the *inherited belief* that their economic ceiling is local, Brown is direct medicine. His core message — that potential is universal but expression is chosen — is precisely the reframe many talented island sellers need to step into national or international careers.

## Legacy

Brown belongs to the small group of speakers whose voices alone produce measurable behavior change in audiences. His career is the proof of his philosophy: an ordinary man, with extraordinary will, can build something remarkable.
