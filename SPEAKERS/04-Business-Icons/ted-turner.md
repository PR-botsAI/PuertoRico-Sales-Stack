# Ted Turner

**Born:** 1938  
**Category:** Business Icon / Media Entrepreneur / Speaker  
**Status:** Founder of CNN, TBS, TNT; one of the largest private landowners in North America; transformative figure in modern media

---

## Core Philosophy

> "Lead, follow, or get out of the way."

Turner's premise: bold, contrarian, *first-mover* bets win disproportionately when others are too cautious. His career is built on entering markets others dismissed as impossible — 24-hour cable news, classic film rebroadcast, large-scale bison ranching — and dominating them.

## Signature Themes

- **First-mover advantage** — CNN created the 24-hour news category. Being first creates a structural moat.
- **Bet on yourself** — Turner repeatedly took personally-guaranteed risks others wouldn't.
- **Long-term capital deployment** — Land, brands, and infrastructure outlast trends.
- **Cause-driven business** — The Turner Foundation, his environmental philanthropy, and his bison restoration are *integrated* with his business identity, not separate from it.

## Speaking Style

Direct, unfiltered, occasionally provocative — Turner is famously plainspoken and unconcerned with corporate diplomacy. His keynotes carry the authority of a man who has made enormous bets and seen them through.

## Why Puerto Rico Entrepreneurs Should Study Turner

Turner's playbook is the masterclass in *category creation*. For PR entrepreneurs thinking about Latin-Caribbean media, content, tourism platforms, or sustainable agriculture, his lessons about being first, betting boldly, and integrating purpose with profit are structural rather than inspirational.

## Legacy

Turner created the modern 24-hour news category and proved that a single bold entrepreneur can rewrite the rules of an industry. His career is a permanent reference text for first-mover strategy.
