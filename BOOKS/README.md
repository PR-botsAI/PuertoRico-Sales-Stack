# 📚 BOOKS

The core library. Organized by era, not alphabet — because the order in which you read these matters.

## Directories

### `01-Foundational-Psychology/`
The bedrock. Books written before "sales" had become a science — back when it was still craft, observation, and field-tested instinct. Every modern framework borrows from these.

- How to Win Friends and Influence People — Dale Carnegie (1936)
- How I Raised Myself from Failure to Success in Selling — Frank Bettger (1947)
- The One Minute Manager — Blanchard & Johnson (1982)
- How to Master the Art of Selling — Tom Hopkins (1980)
- Ziglar on Selling — Zig Ziglar (1991)
- The Psychology of Selling — Brian Tracy (1985)
- Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion — Robert Cialdini (1984)
- SPIN Selling — Neil Rackham (1988)

### `02-Modern-Strategy/`
The operating systems of the current era. Acquisition, prospecting cadence, tactical empathy, offer construction.

- $100M Offers — Alex Hormozi (2021)
- $100M Leads — Alex Hormozi (2023)
- Never Split the Difference — Chris Voss (2016)
- Fanatical Prospecting — Jeb Blount (2015)
- The Challenger Sale — Dixon & Adamson (2011)
- Predictable Revenue — Ross & Tyler (2011)
- To Sell Is Human — Daniel Pink (2012)
- Pitch Anything — Oren Klaff (2011)
- The 10X Rule — Grant Cardone (2011)

### `03-Billionaire-Recommended/`
The titles billionaires and top performers consistently surface in interviews and reading lists. Less about technique, more about how to think.

- Made to Stick — Chip & Dan Heath (2007)
- The Go-Giver — Burg & Mann (2007)
- Go-Givers Sell More — Burg & Mann (2010)
- Mastery — Robert Greene (2012)
- The Ultimate Sales Machine — Chet Holmes (2007)
- The Little Red Book of Selling — Jeffrey Gitomer (2004)

### `04-Additional-Classics/`
Deep cuts. Specialist works that punch above their weight on specific problems.

- The War of Art — Steven Pressfield (2002)
- Integrity Selling for the 21st Century — Ron Willingham (2003)
- Strategic Selling — Miller & Heiman (1985)
- Selling to VITO — Anthony Parinello (1994)
- The Charisma Myth — Olivia Fox Cabane (2012)
- Mastering the Complex Sale — Jeff Thull (2003)
- Cold Calling for Chickens — Bob Etherington (2006)

## Book File Schema

Every book file in this archive follows the same structure:

```markdown
# Title — Author (Year)

## Bibliographic
## Core Thesis
## Key Frameworks
## Principles & Tactics
## Notable Concepts
## Critique & Context
## Puerto Rico Application
## Reading Priority
```

This is intentional. It supports skim-reading, deep study, AI training, and side-by-side comparison.
