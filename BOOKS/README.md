# BOOKS

The canonical reading stack — organized chronologically and by strategic function, spanning early-20th-century relational psychology through 2025 acquisition science.

Three buckets, each with a distinct purpose:

## [01 — Foundational Psychology](./01-Foundational-Psychology/)
*1900s through mid-20th century.* The bedrock works on persuasion, influence, motivation, management, and classic closing technique. These are the books every other book in the archive builds on or reacts against.

- *How to Win Friends and Influence People* — Dale Carnegie
- *Ziglar on Selling* — Zig Ziglar
- *The One Minute Manager* — Ken Blanchard & Spencer Johnson
- *The Psychology of Selling* — Brian Tracy
- *Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion* — Robert B. Cialdini
- *SPIN Selling* — Neil Rackham
- *How I Raised Myself from Failure to Success in Selling* — Frank Bettger
- *How to Master the Art of Selling* — Tom Hopkins

## [02 — Modern Strategy](./02-Modern-Strategy/)
*Late-20th-century strategic operators through 2025 acquisition science.* The methodologies and frameworks deployed by working sellers in 2026, plus the classic-strategic books (Miller-Heiman, Parinello, Holmes, Thull, Etherington) that remain in active use.

- *$100M Offers* — Alex Hormozi
- *$100M Leads* — Alex Hormozi
- *Mastering Influence* — Tony Robbins
- *The Challenger Sale* — Matthew Dixon & Brent Adamson
- *Predictable Revenue* — Aaron Ross & Marylou Tyler
- *To Sell Is Human* — Daniel H. Pink
- *Pitch Anything* — Oren Klaff
- *Never Split the Difference* — Chris Voss
- *The 10X Rule* — Grant Cardone
- *Fanatical Prospecting* — Jeb Blount
- *The Little Red Book of Selling* — Jeffrey Gitomer
- *The Ultimate Sales Machine* — Chet Holmes
- *Strategic Selling* — Miller & Heiman
- *Selling to VITO* — Anthony Parinello
- *Mastering the Complex Sale* — Jeff Thull
- *Cold Calling for Chickens* — Bob Etherington

## [03 — Billionaire Recommended](./03-Billionaire-Recommended/)
*The books top performers and billionaires repeatedly cite.* Not pure tactical sales manuals — the philosophy, communication, and craft books upstream of how the best operators think and present.

- *Made to Stick* — Chip & Dan Heath
- *The Go-Giver* — Bob Burg & John David Mann
- *Go-Givers Sell More* — Bob Burg & John David Mann
- *Mastery* — Robert Greene
- *The Charisma Myth* — Olivia Fox Cabane
- *The War of Art* — Steven Pressfield
- *Integrity Selling for the 21st Century* — Ron Willingham

## File Schema

Every book entry follows the same structure so the archive is predictable for human readers, content creators, and AI fine-tuning pipelines:

1. **Title & Author**
2. **Era & Classification**
3. **Overview**
4. **Core Premise**
5. **Key Concepts** (3–7)
6. **Signature Techniques / Frameworks**
7. **Quotables** (3–5, paraphrased)
8. **Why Top Performers / Billionaires Cite It**
9. **Application in the Puerto Rico Industry**
10. **How to Study This Book**
11. **Cross-References**

## Reading Pathways

- **Beginner seller (90 days):** Carnegie → Ziglar → Tracy → Hopkins → *Little Red Book* → *Fanatical Prospecting* → *$100M Offers* → *Never Split the Difference*.
- **Enterprise / complex sale:** *SPIN Selling* → *Strategic Selling* → *Selling to VITO* → *Mastering the Complex Sale* → *The Challenger Sale*.
- **Modern acquisition / growth operator:** *$100M Offers* → *$100M Leads* → *Predictable Revenue* → *Ultimate Sales Machine* → *Made to Stick*.
- **Presence and character:** *How to Win Friends* → *Mastering Influence* → *The Charisma Myth* → *The War of Art* → *Mastery* → *Integrity Selling*.
- **Value-based / referral-driven:** *The Go-Giver* → *Go-Givers Sell More* → *Integrity Selling* → *To Sell Is Human*.
