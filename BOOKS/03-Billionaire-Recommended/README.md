# 03 — Billionaire & Top-Performer Recommended

*The books that show up repeatedly when you ask people who have built or run nine- and ten-figure businesses what they actually read.*

These aren't pure tactical sales manuals. They're the philosophy, communication, and craft books that top performers cite as the upstream cause of how they think, present, and sell. Many of these are recommended in interviews by figures like Warren Buffett, Jeff Bezos, Mark Cuban, Richard Branson, Tim Ferriss's billionaire guests, and the operator-investors who fund modern sales orgs.

## What's in here

- [**Made to Stick** — Chip Heath & Dan Heath](./made-to-stick.md) — The SUCCESs framework for ideas that endure.
- [**The Go-Giver** — Bob Burg & John David Mann](./the-go-giver.md) — The five laws of stratospheric success.
- [**Go-Givers Sell More** — Bob Burg & John David Mann](./go-givers-sell-more.md) — Operationalizing Go-Giver philosophy in real selling.
- [**Mastery** — Robert Greene](./mastery-greene.md) — The path from apprentice to expert to master.
- [**The Charisma Myth** — Olivia Fox Cabane](./the-charisma-myth.md) — Charisma as a teachable skill, not an inborn trait.
- [**The War of Art** — Steven Pressfield](./the-war-of-art.md) — Resistance, the inner enemy of every seller, founder, and creator.
- [**Integrity Selling for the 21st Century** — Ron Willingham](./integrity-selling.md) — Selling as a values-aligned profession, not a manipulation game.

## Why this bucket exists separately

Most sales books teach what to *say* and *do*. Billionaire-recommended books teach what to *become* — the underlying character, communication standard, and craft posture that makes the said-and-done effective. Carnegie wins your trust; Cialdini wins your assent; Hormozi wins your wallet. The books in this bucket win something different — the buyer's long-term *belief* in the seller as a worth-engaging human.

The distinction matters in Puerto Rico, where most enterprise deals close over many touchpoints and where a seller's reputation persists across the small-market network for a decade or more.

## How to use this section

- **If you want to be remembered:** *Made to Stick*.
- **If your seller identity is stuck in transactional mode:** *The Go-Giver*, *Go-Givers Sell More*, *Integrity Selling*.
- **If you're plateaued and want to break through:** *Mastery*, *The War of Art*.
- **If you're technically strong but presence-weak in the room:** *The Charisma Myth*.

Read these once for the framework, then re-read annually for the recalibration.
