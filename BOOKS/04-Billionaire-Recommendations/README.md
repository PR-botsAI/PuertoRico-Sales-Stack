# 04 — Billionaire & Top-Performer Recommendations

The books that top operators — founders of billion-dollar companies, hedge-fund principals, top-decile founders — consistently cite when asked what genuinely shaped their thinking on selling, communication, and influence.

These aren't the obvious picks. They're the ones that come up in long-form interviews and behind-the-scenes conversations, and they reveal something about how high-leverage operators actually think.
