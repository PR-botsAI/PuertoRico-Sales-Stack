## Core Thesis

If the offer is the conversion engine, **leads are the fuel**. Hormozi catalogs every method of generating qualified strangers' attention into a single integrated framework. The successful business uses all four lead sources strategically and stacks them over time into compounding flywheels.

## The Four Core Lead Methods

1. **Warm Outreach** — People who already know you. The cheapest, fastest, most under-utilized lead source.
2. **Posting Free Content** — Organic audience-building. Slow start, exponential compounding.
3. **Cold Outreach** — Direct contact with strangers. Highest immediate volume, requires offer-market fit.
4. **Paid Ads** — Buying attention. Most scalable, requires the highest offer skill.

## Key Concepts

- **The Core Four sequence:** Most operators master one channel and ignore the others. The compounding effect comes from sequenced expansion.
- **Lead magnets:** Engineer free value that pre-qualifies the right people and disqualifies the wrong ones.
- **The Rule of 100:** 100 outbound actions per day — across the Core Four — produces predictable lead flow regardless of skill level.
- **Affiliates, agencies, employees, customers:** Four lead-generation *teams* to build over time, in that order.

## Application to Modern Sales

- **Warm-outreach reactivation campaigns:** Every business has a list of past customers, dormant prospects, and abandoned cart contacts — worth more than any cold list ever will be.
- **Content as long-game leverage:** A weekly post for 36 months builds a pipeline asset no competitor can copy.
- **Cold + paid synergy:** Paid ads warm cold lists; cold lists fuel paid retargeting.

## Puerto Rico Market Relevance

PR's small geographic and demographic footprint *amplifies* warm outreach. A weekly content discipline on LinkedIn, IG, or YouTube targeting the PR business community produces market-leading inbound flow within a year — because almost no local operators do it consistently. The structural opportunity for first-movers is enormous.

## Key Takeaways

- Four lead sources, sequenced, compound exponentially.
- Warm outreach is the gold mine under everyone's feet.
- Do 100 outbound actions a day. Tactics are secondary to volume.
